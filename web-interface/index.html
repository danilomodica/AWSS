All you have to do is select the two files containing the DNA strings with a .txt extension and then click on the "Compute" button.

When a computation is completed, an email is sent to you containing a code to insert above to immediately obtain a file containing the calculated substring.

The calculated results will remain available for 365 days in case they come in handy.

It depends on how big the starting strings are, in any case, at the moment, jobs can stay active for a maximum of 24 hours, in case the limit is exceeded the job is stopped.

Essentially they must be a string of only adenine, cytosine, guanine and thymine, with no spaces between them, in upper or lower case. E.g. "ATCGCT..."

The service is currently free of charge. The possibility of a paid service with more functions, more computing power and less time limitations is not excluded in the future.
